Michael Jackson

Short Description

Michael Jackson (August 29, 1958 – June 25, 2009) was an American singer, dancer, and songwriter, known as the “King of Pop.” He began his career as a member of the Jackson 5 alongside his brothers and quickly became a global superstar.

In the 1980s and 1990s, Jackson released several iconic albums, including Thriller, Bad, and Dangerous, which broke sales records and established him as one of the most influential artists of all time. He was also famous for his innovative music videos, unique dance moves like the “moonwalk,” and outstanding live performances.

Beyond music, Michael Jackson was involved in philanthropy, supporting children and people affected by poverty and illness around the world. Despite his enormous success, his life was also marked by controversies and legal battles, making him a highly discussed public figure.

Timeline of Life

  • 1958 – Born in Gary, Indiana, USA
  • 1964 – Joined The Jackson 5
  • 1971 – Began solo career
  • 1982 – Released Thriller album
  • 1987 – Released Bad album
  • 1993 – Married Lisa Marie Presley
  • 2009 – Passed away in Los Angeles, California, USA
